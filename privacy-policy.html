PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: JUNE 27, 2026

WELCOME TO GuessTheFlag, POWERED BY SIRAT TECHNOLOGIES. WE BELIEVE YOUR TRIVIA INTERESTS AND GAMING PROGRESS SHOULD REMAIN PRIVATE. THIS POLICY OUTLINES OUR COMMITMENT TO YOUR DATA SECURITY.

OUR COMMITMENT: GuessTheFlag IS BUILT ON A "LOCAL-FIRST" DATA MODEL. WE DO NOT COLLECT YOUR HIGH SCORES, COMPLETED LEVELS, XP, OR INTERACTION HISTORY ON OUR SERVERS. YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

GuessTheFlag IS A COUNTRY FLAG TRIVIA GAME DESIGNED TO RUN SEAMLESSLY ON YOUR MOBILE DEVICE.

  • NO CLOUD ACCOUNTS: WE DO NOT REQUIRE AN EMAIL OR SOCIAL MEDIA LOGIN. YOUR IDENTITY IS NEVER LINKED TO YOUR GAMEPLAY.
  • GAME PROGRESS LOGS: DATA REGARDING YOUR COMPLETED LEVELS, UNLOCKED STAGES, AND XP PROGRESS ARE STORED EXCLUSIVELY ON YOUR LOCAL DEVICE STORAGE USING ASYNCSTORAGE.
  • DYNAMIC CONTENT REQUESTS (FLAG IMAGES): THE APP DYNAMICALLY DOWNLOADS COUNTRY FLAGS FROM A PUBLIC CONTENT DELIVERY NETWORK (CDN) AT https://flagcdn.com. WHEN FETCHING THESE IMAGES, STANDARD TECHNICAL ACCESS LOGS (SUCH AS YOUR DEVICE'S IP ADDRESS) MAY BE PROCESSED TEMPORARILY BY THE CDN PROVIDER SOLELY TO SERVE THE IMAGE FILE. THESE ASSETS ARE CACHED ON YOUR DEVICE SO THAT REPEATED REQUESTS ARE MINIMIZED.
  • NO BEHAVIORAL ANALYTICS: WE DO NOT USE ANALYTICS SDKS TO TRACK HOW OFTEN YOU PLAY, WHICH FLAGS YOU GUESS INCORRECTLY, OR HOW LONG YOU SPEND IN EACH LEVEL.

2. PERMISSIONS

WE REQUEST STANDARD NETWORK PERMISSIONS SOLELY FOR THE APP TO DOWNLOAD COUNTRY FLAG IMAGES FROM THE PUBLIC CDN. WE DO NOT REQUEST ACCESS TO SENSITIVE DEVICE DATA SUCH AS LOCATION, CONTACTS, PHOTOS, CAMERA, OR NOTIFICATIONS.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

TO ENSURE A FOCUSSED, ADS-FREE, AND PREMIUM TRIVIA EXPERIENCE, GuessTheFlag DOES NOT INCLUDE ADVERTISING SDKS, BEHAVIORAL TRACKERS, DATA BROKER INTEGRATIONS, OR SOCIAL MEDIA PLUGINS.

4. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

OUR APP IS SAFE FOR TRIVIA ENTHUSIASTS OF ALL AGES. BECAUSE WE DO NOT COLLECT, TRANSMIT, OR STORE PERSONAL DATA, WE ARE FULLY COMPLIANT WITH COPPA AND INTERNATIONAL PRIVACY STANDARDS.
